Some Words on Music

I have played, composed, and dreamed through music for much of my life. I am blessed to be exploring the piano and improving my skills fairly often. My interest in making music right now is mostly for the joys of expression and learning, so I don't have plans at this time to formally organize the music. That may change later; who knows? Music is momentary for me these days. It flows through me, and generally flows out. It's becoming more and more rare for me to mold melodies and music into full-fledged songs (that used to be the only way I'd write). These days I just explore, learning as I play.

The easiest things for me to share right now are the two EPs I wrote under the musical moniker Brightnine (which I have since abandoned). Here is a link to the first EP, the Space EP, and another to the second, Songs to Burn to.

I am also working on some Musical Practice Tools, which anyone may use to improve the way they explore and practice musical instruments. Those should be shared on this website when they are in a workable state.
